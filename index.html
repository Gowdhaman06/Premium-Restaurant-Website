About L'Étoile

Our Story & Heritage

The Legacy of Taste

Established in 2018, L'Étoile was founded with the aspiration to create a sensory escape. We sought to decouple fine dining from stuffy traditions, shifting focus purely to culinary genius, warm service, and luxurious contemporary environment.

Every ingredient is sourced from sustainable micro-farms, ensuring maximum flavor clarity and supporting local biodiversity. Each dish tells a story of origin, technique, and creative flair.

2018

The Opening

L'Étoile opens its massive mahogany doors in Manhattan's historic district, introducing our signature progressive style.

2020

First Michelin Star

The culinary world awards our precise techniques and unique botanical flavor profiles our first Michelin Star.

2023

A Second Star & Global Acclaim

Recognized for our zero-waste sustainability initiatives and continuous boundary-pushing pairings.

Reservation Guidelines

To offer an unparalleled dining experience, reservations are strictly managed. Please review our instructions before booking.

  • Cancellation Policy Cancellations must be made 48 hours in advance. Late cancellations will be subject to a fee of $50 per person.
  • Dress Code Smart elegant dress code is required. Athletic wear, shorts, and flip-flops are not permitted.
  • Children Policy We welcome children over the age of 12. Children must order from the standard tasting menu.
  • Group Reservations For parties larger than 8, please contact our events team at +1 (212) 555-0199.
